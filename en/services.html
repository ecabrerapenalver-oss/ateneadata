Services

What we solve

We cover the full Power BI cycle: from initial diagnosis to continuous operation. Each service addresses a specific business problem.

01

Power BI Diagnosis

Full audit of your current environment. We assess what works, what fails and what's missing. We review data model, performance, security, adoption and quality of existing reports. The result is a clear map of where you are and a prioritized roadmap of where to go.

Problem it solves

You don't know if your Power BI environment is properly set up, if the data is reliable or if you're leveraging the tool.

Deliverable

Complete status report + prioritized roadmap with actionable recommendations.

Who it's for

Companies already using Power BI but unsure if it's properly set up or wanting to take it to the next level.

02

Executive reporting

We design dashboards with business criteria. It's not about adding charts: it's about defining what questions each report answers, for whom, how it's read and what decisions it enables. The reading experience is designed from leadership's perspective, not the technician's.

Problem it solves

You have dashboards nobody uses, reports that don't answer what leadership needs or a confusing reading experience.

Deliverable

Functional Power BI dashboards + executive reading guide + key KPI definition.

Who it's for

C-suite, finance or operations leadership who need to see their business clearly and make data-driven decisions.

03

Data modeling & architecture

We build the semantic model underneath the reports: tables, relationships, DAX metrics, calculation logic. What makes data reliable, consistent and the environment maintainable and scalable without becoming chaotic.

Problem it solves

Metrics that don't match, unmaintainable models, poor performance or calculations nobody understands.

Deliverable

Documented, optimized Power BI data model built on best practices.

Who it's for

Companies that need a solid, maintainable data foundation — not patches on an improvised model.

04

Governance & platform

We define and configure everything that turns Power BI into a governed platform: roles, security, workspaces, deployment pipelines, access policies and standards. So it's not a chaos of shared files, but a controlled environment.

Problem it solves

Multiple users accessing without control, duplicate reports, unprotected sensitive data or error-prone manual deployments.

Deliverable

Configured Power BI environment + governance documentation + security and access policies.

Who it's for

Companies with multiple areas or users consuming data that need order, security and scalability.

05

Power BI rescue

For environments built without criteria that are now a problem. Data that doesn't add up, reports nobody trusts, unacceptable performance, unmaintainable models. We audit, diagnose and rebuild on a solid foundation.

Problem it solves

Your Power BI environment exists but doesn't work. Users don't trust it, performance is poor and nobody wants to touch it.

Deliverable

Rebuilt environment on a solid foundation + documentation + transition from the previous setup.

Who it's for

Companies that invested in Power BI but the result doesn't meet expectations and need things done right.

06

Continuous operation

Maintenance, evolution, support and continuous improvement of your Power BI environment. This is what turns Power BI into a permanent capability of your company without needing to hire internal staff. We ensure your environment improves over time, not degrades.

Problem it solves

You don't have internal staff to maintain Power BI, reports become outdated and nobody evolves the environment.

Deliverable

Dedicated external team + continuous support + monthly environment evolution. Fixed monthly fee.

Who it's for

Companies that want Power BI running and improving without managing a dedicated internal technical team.
